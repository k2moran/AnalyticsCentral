Analytics & Data Advisory Council

The Analytics and Data Advisory Council (ADAC) provides oversight of URMC's data and analytics strategy to support decision-making and alignment with the University of Rochester Medical Center business objectives. This Advisory Council ensures the integrated Analytics Community of Practice (CoP) roadmap, budget and policies align to initiatives that support the organization's prioritized strategic goals.

ADAC Purpose & Authority

Delegated Authority

  • Delegated from the URMC IT Executive Leadership Council
  • Authorized to delegate or revoke powers to workgroups
  • Can approve data governance policies
  • Reviews and approves Data & Analytics Community of Practice Roadmap
  • Approves fiscal budget recommendations for data management technology investments

Key Responsibilities

  • Approve Charters for all associated Workgroups, Centers of Excellence and Community of Practice
  • Review and approve Data & Analytics Community of Practice Roadmap
  • Review and approve fiscal budget recommendations for data management technology investments
  • Approve URMC data governance policies
  • Provide oversight for operational readiness for workflow changes associated with new technology implementations
  • Review Key Performance Indicators for analytics program effectiveness

Governance Hierarchy

Analytics & Data Advisory Council (ADAC)

Oversight of URMC's data and analytics strategy

Community of Practice (CoP)

Fostering federated cooperation among analytics groups

Strategic Workgroups

Long-term, enterprise-wide projects

Hospital & Health System Project Workgroup

Enhancing system-wide hospital operations

Service Line Strategy Project Workgroup

Improving service line efficiency and driving growth

Research & Innovation Project Workgroup

Expanding research & innovation collaboration

Tactical Workgroups

Immediate, ad-hoc requests and "fires"

Clinical Operations Request Workgroup

Handling urgent clinical operations analytics needs

Finance and Operations Request Workgroup

Managing urgent administrative analytics needs

Governance Principles

Strategic Direction

The ADAC Council sets overall strategy and provides governance oversight for all analytics initiatives, ensuring alignment with organizational goals.

Decision-Making

Decisions require majority plus one (51%) for approval. All workgroups follow similar governance processes for project evaluation and prioritization.

Resource Allocation

Strategic workgroups receive planned allocation based on priorities, while tactical workgroups have dynamic allocation based on urgency.

Key Deliverables & Outcomes

ADAC Deliverables

  • URMC Data & Analytics Roadmap

    Annual schematic of operational initiatives and enabling technologies demonstrating alignment with URMC business objectives

  • Key Performance Indicators

    Quarterly metrics that determine the effectiveness of analytics programs, used to drive optimization and solution prioritization

  • Workgroup Charter Approvals

    Approval of charters for all associated workgroups as needed, ensuring proper governance and alignment

  • Data & Analytics Policy Approval

    Review and approval of policies governing usage of data and technology solutions by URMC staff

Workgroup Deliverables

  • Project Prioritization Recommendations

    Strategic evaluation and ranking of projects based on organizational priorities and available resources

  • Meeting Minutes & Decisions

    Documentation of key decisions, approvals, and action items from workgroup meetings

  • Status Reports

    Regular updates on active projects and requests, tracking progress against timelines and deliverables

  • Enterprise Data Opportunity Recommendations

    Identification of strategic opportunities for enhancing data assets and analytics capabilities
