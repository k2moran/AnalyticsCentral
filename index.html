Our Data & Analytics Vision

At the University of Rochester Medical Center, we have implemented a comprehensive analytics strategy that integrates enterprise governance, federated development capabilities, and specialized clinical expertise. Our goal is to create a unified analytics ecosystem that delivers actionable insights to the right people at the right time, enabling data-driven excellence in patient care, operations, and research.

Vision

Create a unified analytics ecosystem that delivers actionable insights to the right people at the right time, enabling data-driven excellence in patient care, operations, and research.

Mission

Provide an integrated analytics platform with federated development capabilities that empower departments while maintaining enterprise data governance, security, and standards.

Strategic Goals

  • Create a single source of truth for critical enterprise data
  • Enable federated analytics capabilities with governance
  • Embed analytics in clinical and operational workflows
  • Foster a data-driven organizational culture

How Our Analytics Plans Work Together

Our analytics strategy integrates multiple components to create a comprehensive ecosystem that supports all aspects of URMC's operations:

  • Strategic Governance: The Analytics and Data Advisory Council (ADAC) provides executive oversight and strategic direction, ensuring alignment with organizational priorities.
  • Technical Infrastructure: Our Enterprise Analytics Architecture establishes a robust foundation of integrated data sources, warehouses, and virtualization technologies.
  • Collaborative Community: Our Community of Practice (CoP) and Centers of Excellence (CoEs) create a federated model that balances enterprise standards with specialized expertise.
  • Clinical Integration: Clinical Analytics Hubs enable specialty-specific insights while maintaining alignment with enterprise standards.
  • Research Support: Dedicated research data resources and governance processes facilitate both clinical and translational research initiatives.
